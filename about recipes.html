About Recipes

Copycat Bread

This recipe is an easy version of garlic breadstick that can be made in a couple of hours.
The original recipe is by Morgan Baker from The Spruce Eats.
The Total time it takes: 3 hrs 1 mins
Prep time: 25 mins
Cook time: 16 mins
Rest time: 2 hrs 20 mins

Bun Bread

This is one of the best buns with an easy recipe. It is light and fluffy but still sturdy.
The original recipe is by Lauryn Bodden from The Spruce Eats.
The Total time it takes: 4 hrs 10 min
Prep time: 25 mins
Cook time: 15 mins
Proof: 3 hrs 30 mins

Bundt Cake

This chocolate chip bundt cake is an easy recipe to make. It is made with sour cream, a little cinnamon, and a thick layer of chocolate ganache.
The original recipe is by Janice Lawandi from Bake School.
The Total time it takes: 1 hour hr 35minutes mins
Prep Time: 20minutes mins
